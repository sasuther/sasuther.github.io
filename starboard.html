Starboard

"Starboard is an immersive sailing adventure game made for the HTC Vive and Oculus Rift."
This game was made in 36 hours for KSU Game Jam 2016 with 3 team members.

I created the creature and ship models, and rigged and animated the creature models. I also wrote some of the gameplay scripts.

Made with: HTC Vive, Unity, C#, Maya, Blender, Photoshop, Illustrator

Winner: Best VR Game and selected to present at FutureXLive
