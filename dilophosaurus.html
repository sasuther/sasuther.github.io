Dilophosaurus Dash

"Dilophosaurus Dash is a 2-player running game. As the dilophosaurus, run for your life to get to your cave! As the T-Rex, chase down that little egg thief! Be careful though, you could switch characters at any time. Collect three bones to be able to instantly switch characters."
This game was made in 48 hours for Chillenium Game Jam 2017 with 4 team members.

I modeled, rigged, and animated the characters, as well as made all particle effects and wrote some scripts.

Made with: Unity, C#, Maya, Blender, Photoshop, Illustrator
