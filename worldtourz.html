World Tour Z

"In World Tour Z you progress through a zombie clown infested apocalypse by pedaling. The game registers how fast you're pedaling and the bike in game will speed up or slow down accordingly. But if that isn't enough for you you're also equipped with a .45 that will help you deal with those pesky undead clowns. Remember! Each clip only has 10 rounds, so make sure to reach to your side to reload. Look down at your bike screen to keep up with your stats. This game is procedurally generated, so you will never run out of zombie clowns!"
This game was made in 36 hours for UGA Hacks 2017 with 4 team members.

I modeled, rigged, and animated the characters and wrote some of the game scripts.

Made with: HTC Vive, Unity, C#, Maya, Photoshop, Illustrator

Winner: Third Place Overall
